Create Active Learning about the Weather

You can use the following educational simulation and tools to explore meteorological concepts like solar and terrestrial radiation, humidity variables, temperature lags and adiabatic processes. Tools can be used to draw weather maps, sort clouds etc.

Each simulation or tool is written in HTML5/CSS/JS and runs in both browsers and mobile.

MtnSim

Adjust the initial temperature and vapor pressure of a parcel, denoted by a leaf, to observe how saturation occurs as the parcel travels over the mountain. The challenge is observe and hypothesize how these parameters affect when and where saturation occurs to identify the variety of paths to saturation.
